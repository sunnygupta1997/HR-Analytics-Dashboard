📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee data and identify key factors influencing attrition and workforce trends. The dashboard helps in understanding employee behavior and supports data-driven HR decisions.

---

📁 Dataset Features

- Employee ID
- Age & Age Group
- Department & Job Role
- Education Field
- Monthly Income
- Job Satisfaction
- Years at Company
- Attrition Status

---

📊 Key Insights

- Total Employees: 1470

- Attrition Count: 237

- Attrition Rate: 16.12%

- Average Salary: 6.5K

- Higher attrition observed in specific age groups

- Job role and salary significantly impact attrition

- Employee satisfaction varies across departments

---

📈 Dashboard Highlights

- Attrition by Age Group
- Attrition by Years at Company
- Attrition by Education Field
- Job Satisfaction Analysis
- Interactive Filters & Slicers

---

🛠 Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel


---

🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard provides valuable insights into employee attrition and helps organizations take proactive steps to improve employee retention and overall workforce management.
